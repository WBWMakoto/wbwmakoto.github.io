Q&A

0. What is Not Pixel and can it help me earn some "breakfast"? How can I join Not Pixel?

Not Pixel is a mini-app within Telegram where you can participate in pixel art creation to earn points and win exciting rewards. Specifically, Season 2 of Not Pixel focuses on an "invest to win and earn" mechanism.

Regarding whether you can "earn some breakfast," Not Pixel operates on a play-to-earn (P2E) model, where players earn PX points through in-game activities. These points have the potential to be converted into cryptocurrency. However, the actual ability to "earn a living" from P2E projects depends on many factors such as the token's value, market volatility, and your level of participation.

You can join Not Pixel instantly by clicking here: https://t.me/notpixel/app?startapp=f5822071900_s577266

1. Why are some data showing as incorrect / not available?

The reason some data might appear incorrect or unavailable could be due to one of the following:

  • API Update Error: There might be an issue with the data update process from the Not Pixel app's API.
  • Not Pixel Team Hiding Stats: The Not Pixel development team might have decided to temporarily or permanently hide certain statistics.
  • Loading: In some cases, please allow up to 15 seconds for the website to fully load. (In 95% of tests, the website loads completely in under 5 seconds.)

Estimated Data: Some data are estimated (marked with "est"). These values may not be 100% accurate, but I guarantee the maximum error is within 1% compared to the actual value.
